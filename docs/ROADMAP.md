# Roadmap

This roadmap is intentionally short and current. Items may change as public-data
availability, store requirements, and safety review findings evolve.

## Near Term

- Keep CI, CodeQL, OpenSSF Scorecard, and SonarCloud signals healthy.
- Complete OpenSSF Best Practices checklist evidence through Gold where the
  project can truthfully satisfy the criteria.
- Improve Android and iOS packaging reproducibility and release signing notes.
- Expand tests for authentication, watch pairing, public-data fallback, and
  safety dashboard aggregation.

## Medium Term

- Add documented release provenance for signed APKs and source releases.
- Improve accessibility contrast, keyboard navigation, and mobile screen-reader
  behavior.
- Strengthen privacy controls for location retention and deletion requests.
- Add deployment documentation for PostgreSQL/PostGIS production setups.

## Not Planned Without Explicit Review

- Advertising SDKs, analytics SDKs, or proprietary tracking dependencies.
- Public exposure of precise individual location or health data.
- Claims of official 119 dispatch integration without verified integration.
- Storage of production secrets, real emergency contacts, or real user tracks in
  the repository.
